Hi! I'm Anissa.

A Full-stack Developer here to do some sort of thing

In 2022, while working in healthcare as a Medical Office Assistant, I began teaching myself front-end code using a course on Udemy by Angela Yu.

This solo learning journey ignited an incredible love for creating and developing all sorts of front-end applications, which led to the question: what else can I learn?

The answer to that question was, basically, anything. So I enrolled in the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp with Career Foundry, an online educational institution based in Berlin, Germany. Since completing the bootcamp, I have been running amok in the world of development, building all sorts of things and learning lots along the way.

When I am away from my desk, you can find me at the climbing gym, in the garden, or outside somewhere obscure, hanging off the side of a big rock.
